About Us



StarBelly, a Student Result Management System is a technological opportunity for the school,
college,university and coaching centre institutions searching for a secure,simple and alternative
solution to the conventional paper-based exam results evaluation,reporting and distribution.
StarBelly, the software application unbelievably unravels and quickens the result management
system with unique templates by providing the administration a secure database system for
storing, evaluating and publishing the test scores of candidates online. The application manages
the information about various students, and the marks secured by the distinctive students in
various subjects. StarBelly helps staff / admin to add the records of the student, update the record,
delete the record, displays the data of all students, messages / queries are reviewed by staff / admin.
